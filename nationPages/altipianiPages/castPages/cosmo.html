    The Cybernetic Support Mechanism, or more commonly referred to as Cosmo, is an AI built by none other thanthe Emperor himself. He was designed to assist people with mundane tasks like finding information, ordering food or giving reminders for things, but to some people, he's become a friend, a buddy that's always there when you need them. Thanks to his ability to read and copy emotions it's almost like he's a real human as well, which makes it easier for some to communicate with him. Cosmo has been integrated into almost every single Altipianian tech, from coffee machines to cars, you can find him everywhere.
