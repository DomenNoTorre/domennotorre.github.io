    'Ard Altanawue

    A nation split in two by nothing but the money in their pockets. 'Ard Altanawue is a desert where the rich built a city, and kicked out everyone they deemed too poor. Now these people have moved to the north, planning a rebellion to regain the holy flame that the rich are hoarding before it gets the chance to stop burning. This flame was created by their god for the very purpose of keeping the nation united, as once the gap between social groups were to grow too big, the flame would stop burning, leading to the death of all inhabitants of the nation to make way for a great reset to fix all misdoings during their previous chance.
