    Books

    Our main attraction is our books, we started writing these in 2024 and they are currently still ongoing. Mindbound exists of a collection of books, starting with the main series that follow Nicolas and a few of the other characters he meets along the way, the main series is spread across around 5 books.After the main series we also have “Silent stories”, this is a collection of smaller stories that happen in the world of Mindbound, but wouldn't fit in the main series.Lastly we have plans to make another book besides those, where we will be able to share our scrapped ideas for Mindbound. If you are interested in reading any of these, you can click the links below!

    Eperilet

    Mindbound takes place in Eperilet, a large continent surrounded by water that hosts 16 individual nations. All of this actually first started out as a minecraft world back in 2023! We used the map to come up with the first few nations and after a while expanded it to what it is now. It's about 4643 years old by the time the main story takes place. Over the years a lot has happen, Shokuminchi Kokka taking over Viharkalan, a union being formed with all the nations, the rune wars and of course the tower appearing in Midprot only a year before the main story begins. Each nations has its own culture and lore, so if you're curious about them, click the link below and you'll be taken to their pages!
