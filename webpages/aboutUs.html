    Heya! We're Kyara and Kai and we are the creators of Mindbound! We're currently still students and we always try to write more for Mindbound in between deadlines and exams. So we apologize in advance for the slow updates :')

    Anyways! We're working hard on the main series of Mindbound, though, we're also working on some side stories as we speak! This will be a series we call The Silent Stories, and will focus on things outside of the main series which are canon, just not written about in detail. For example the infamous rune war, or what happened before the main story! We have a bunch of ideas, but we just need to work them all out haha.

    A little fun fact about Mindbound actually, this all started out as a simple minecraft server! We were messing around with lore until we came up with what is now the story we're writing. We didn't expect to eventually post it online though, nor did we expect the support we got from fans! We're gradually expanding this little universe of ours, starting with this website and our discord server! (Which you can join by clicking the button at the end of the page!) We'll keep this site updated, adding new characters, lore, maybe even some easter eggs here and there.

    For now, this is just a fun project for us to work on in our free time and it means a lot to us that so many of you liked it so far. We'd like to thank all our fans who stick around as we write, even if new chapters may take a while to finish haha.
